🌀
Brú na Bóinne / Newgrange — 15 minutes from the hotel. UNESCO World Heritage megalithic passage tomb, 3200 BC — older than the pyramids. Visitor centre + guided tomb tour: 2–3 hours. Book online well in advance — August slots sell out weeks ahead.
✈️
Dublin Airport — 35 minutes from Drogheda. Return hire car, check in for FR816. With a 20:20 departure, aim to arrive by 17:30. No rush.
🎢
Emerald Park (formerly Tayto Park) (optional — trip highlight for the kids) — Ireland's main theme park with rollercoasters, a waterpark, zoo and falconry. In Ashbourne, 20 mins from Drogheda and 25 mins from Dublin Airport. With a 20:20 flight you could spend 3–4 hours here and still make the airport comfortably. Book online in advance.
